About me

A full-stack developer who cares about the craft behind the product — clean architecture, real performance, and interfaces people actually enjoy using.

The journey. I started in Tokyo with a BS in Computer Science from Temple University's Japan campus, then earned an MSc in Computer Science from Birmingham City University with Distinction. Along the way I moved from IoT prototypes to shipping mobile apps used in the real world.

What I do now. I'm a React Native developer building cross-platform apps backed by Node.js APIs and serverless AWS. I've led frontend and backend work, mentored junior developers, and tuned systems for speed — cutting API response times by 60% and load times by 40%.

How I work. I like problems that span the stack: a clean mobile UI, a resilient API behind it, and the cloud plumbing that keeps it fast and cheap. I'm just as comfortable with a machine-learning pipeline as a CI/CD one.
